Job Role Key Responsibilities Salary Range (INR) Job Description
Digital Marketing Manager Overseeing overall digital marketing strategy, managing teams, analyzing campaign performance 8-15 Lakhs
  • Develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing strategies
  • Manage and lead a team of digital marketing professionals
  • Analyze marketing metrics to track performance and identify areas for improvement
  • Collaborate with other departments to ensure brand consistency
SEO Specialist Optimizing websites for search engines, keyword research, link building 5-10 Lakhs
  • Conduct keyword research to identify relevant keywords
  • Optimize website content, meta tags, and headers
  • Build high-quality backlinks to improve website authority
  • Monitor and analyze website performance
Social Media Manager Managing social media channels, creating content, engaging with the audience 4-8 Lakhs
  • Create and curate engaging social media content
  • Manage social media channels and respond to comments and messages
  • Run social media advertising campaigns
  • Analyze social media metrics to measure performance
Content Marketing Manager Developing and implementing content strategies, creating high-quality content 5-12 Lakhs
  • Develop and execute content marketing strategies
  • Create high-quality content such as blog posts, articles, and whitepapers
  • Manage content calendars and editorial calendars
  • Measure content performance and optimize accordingly
PPC Specialist Managing pay-per-click advertising campaigns on platforms like Google Ads and social media 5-10 Lakhs
  • Create and manage PPC campaigns on platforms like Google Ads and social media
  • Optimize campaigns for maximum ROI
  • Track and analyze campaign performance
Email Marketing Specialist Creating and executing email marketing campaigns, analyzing performance 4-8 Lakhs
  • Create and send email campaigns
  • Segment email lists and personalize messages
  • Analyze email performance metrics
  • Automate email marketing workflows
Web Analyst Analyzing website traffic, user behavior, and campaign performance 6-12 Lakhs
  • Use analytics tools to track website performance
  • Analyze user behavior and identify trends
  • Generate reports and insights for marketing teams
Influencer Marketing Manager Identifying and collaborating with influencers to promote brands 5-10 Lakhs
  • Identify relevant influencers for brand partnerships
  • Negotiate and manage influencer campaigns
  • Track influencer performance and ROI
